The Kryptopedia Team

Kryptopedia was founded and is maintained by a diverse group of blockchain enthusiasts, developers, economists, and privacy advocates united by a shared vision of decentralized knowledge. While our platform emphasizes ideas over identities, we believe in transparency about who's building this resource.

Core Team

Alex Nakagawa

Co-Founder & Lead Developer

Alex has been developing blockchain systems since 2013, having contributed to multiple Bitcoin Core releases and founded two previous crypto startups. With a background in distributed systems and cryptography, Alex architects Kryptopedia's technical infrastructure with a focus on decentralization and security.

Before Kryptopedia, Alex worked as a security researcher specializing in cryptocurrency exchanges and smart contract auditing. They hold degrees in Computer Science and Mathematics.

Dr. Wei Zhang

Economic Systems Architect

Wei designed Kryptopedia's token economics and reputation systems, creating the incentive structures that drive our platform. With a PhD in Game Theory and a background in Austrian Economics, Wei ensures that our economic mechanisms align user incentives with content quality.
